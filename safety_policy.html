Health & Safety Policy

Give My Certificate is committed to ensuring the health, safety, and well-being of our employees, contractors, visitors, and anyone affected by our operations.

Scope:

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors at our facilities and during any business-related activities.

Principles:

Compliance: Adherence to all relevant health and safety legislation and regulations.

Safe Environment: Maintain a safe working environment through regular risk assessments and safety audits.

Training: Provide comprehensive health and safety training to all employees.

Incident Reporting: - Encourage prompt reporting of all health and safety incidents, hazards, and near-misses.

Responsibilities:

Management: Ensure resources and support are available for effective health and safety management.

Employees: Follow health and safety procedures and report any concerns.

Implementation:

Risk Management: Identify and mitigate health and safety risks in the workplace.

Emergency Procedures: Establish and communicate clear emergency response procedures.

Commitment:

We are dedicated to continuous improvement in our health and safety practices and regularly review and update our policies.

Conclusion:

We expect all employees and associates to prioritize health and safety, ensuring a safe and healthy working environment for everyone.
