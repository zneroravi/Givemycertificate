General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Policy

For Enterprise Customers

Effective Date: [1 January 2024]
Last Updated: [1 January 2025]

At Give My Certificate (https://givemycertificate.com), one of our highest priorities is protecting the privacy of our users, especially our Enterprise clients. This GDPR Privacy Policy explains the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

If you have any questions or require more information, please contact us at support@givemycertificate.com.

1. Data Controller

SERVICEAMIGOS CERTIFICATE PRIVATE LIMITED is the Data Controller of your personal information.

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • You have entered into a contract with us.
  • You have given us explicit permission.
  • Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests.
  • We are required to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

2. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. We may retain and use your information to:

  • Fulfill our legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements and policies

3. Your Data Protection Rights (EEA Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification – Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure – Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object – Object to our processing of your data.
  • Right to Restriction – Request limitation of data processing in specific cases.
  • Right to Data Portability – Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact support@givemycertificate.com.

4. Log Files

Give My Certificate follows standard procedures for using log files. These files may include:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Click data

This information is not linked to personally identifiable information. It is used for analyzing trends, site administration, user movement tracking, and demographic insights.

5. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many websites, we use cookies to:

  • Store user preferences
  • Track visited pages
  • Enhance and personalize the user experience based on browser and device

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Refer to your browser’s help documentation for details.

6. Third-Party Technologies

Some third-party advertisers or service providers on our website may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons. These may collect your IP address and browser data directly via your browser.

Note:

  • We do not control these cookies.
  • Review third-party privacy policies to understand their practices and opt-out options.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has submitted personal data, contact us immediately at support@givemycertificate.com for prompt removal.

8. Scope of This Policy

This GDPR policy applies only to online activities and is valid for visitors to our website regarding information shared and/or collected via https://givemycertificate.com. It does not apply to information collected offline or through other channels.

9. Contact for GDPR Requests or Complaints

Email us at support@givemycertificate.com with the subject line:
“General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) inquiry/complaint/request”

We aim to respond within 7 working days.
